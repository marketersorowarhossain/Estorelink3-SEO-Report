6-MONTH PRODUCT RANKING ENHANCEMENT TIMELINE (MONTH BY MONTH)

Month 1: Technical Foundation & Leakage Resolution (High Priority)

The first 30 days are dedicated to solving immediate technical leakages, establishing accurate tracking, and laying the groundwork for the Router Hub.

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Comprehensive setup specifically configured for Retargeting and tracking the exact points of consumer drop-off (leakage analysis).
  • Site Performance: Deep technical audit and resolution of low loading speeds (Core Web Vitals) to prevent immediate bounce rates, especially for the 93% mobile user base.
  • Product Floating: Fixing site upload and product display issues to eliminate technical leakages that prevent users from adding items to their cart.
  • Superior User Experience (UX) Optimization: Modern SEO is not just about keywords; it's about performance. Improving site speed, mobile responsiveness, and intuitive UI/UX (like the dedicated Router Hub page) directly correlates with higher rankings and, more importantly, a frictionless path to purchase for the customer.
  • Baseline Keyword Audit: Identifying the initial 30 high-volume networking keywords to target.

Month 2: Content Architecture & On-Page Optimization

Transitioning from technical fixes to front-facing content and user engagement.

  • Breadcrumb Tracking: Technical implementation via Google Tag Manager (GTM) to improve Google's crawling efficiency, enhance search snippet visibility, and track user navigation.
  • SKU Setup: Structuring the inventory meta-data to ensure all current and future product codes (for 150,000+ targeted SKUs) are perfectly indexed by search engines.
  • Router Hub Deployment: Officially launching the dedicated "Router Hub" landing page, fully integrated with the UX improvements made in Month 1.
  • Keyword Mapping & Meta Tags: Writing and deploying optimized Title Tags, Meta Descriptions, and Header (H1, H2) tags for all primary categories.
  • Mobile-First Alignment: Finalizing page element adjustments to ensure zero friction during the browsing and checkout process on mobile devices.
  • XML Sitemap Submission: Pushing the newly structured SKU URLs to Google Search Console to force rapid indexing.

Month 3: Semantic SEO & Content Expansion

Expanding the site's footprint to capture a wider variety of search queries.

  • Keyword Scaling: Expanding the target keyword list from the baseline 17 keywords to 300+ long-tail and specific product queries (e.g., "Best high-speed router in BD").
  • Content Gap Fulfillment: Creating supporting category descriptions and buying guides to build topical authority around networking gear.
  • Internal Linking Strategy: Connecting high-authority pages to new product pages to distribute "link juice" and help Google discover new SKUs faster.

Month 4: Authority Building & Brand Trust (Off-Page SEO)

Building the site's reputation to compete with giants like StarTech and Ryans.

  • E-E-A-T Implementation: Optimizing the "About Us" and customer service pages to leverage the brand's existing reputation, establishing Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness.
  • High-Tier Backlink Acquisition: Securing high-quality, relevant backlinks from tech publishers, PR releases, and industry-specific domains to boost the overall Domain Rating (DR).
  • Competitor Interception: Analyzing the specific search queries where competitors currently dominate, and aggressively optimizing eStore pages to overtake those rankings.

Month 5: Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) & AOV Boost

ensuring the acquired traffic is converting into high-value sales.

  • High-Value Targeting: Shifting SEO focus toward high-margin, enterprise-level products to directly support the forecasted Average Order Value (AOV) growth.
  • Behavioral Adjustments (A/B Testing): Using GTM and GA4 data to run A/B tests on product and checkout pages, ensuring organic traffic converts to actual Gross Merchandise Value (GMV).
  • Retargeting Synergy: Feeding the newly acquired organic traffic data into the GA4 retargeting lists to support future highly-targeted, low-cost marketing campaigns.

Month 6: ROI Scaling & Business Projection Alignment

Reviewing performance and preparing the architecture for the next phase of exponential growth.

  • Performance Auditing: Comparing Month 6 organic traffic, keyword rankings, and revenue against the Month 1 baseline to demonstrate the exact ROI of the SEO campaign.
  • Automated Inventory Scaling: Automating the SKU SEO process so the site remains technically sound as it scales toward the 150,000 SKU target.
  • Next-Phase Strategy: Establishing the roadmap for Quarter 3 and Quarter 4 to ensure the site hits the 5M+ BDT GMV target through sustainable, compounding organic growth.

The ROI Formula:

SEO ROI = (SEO Revenue – SEO Costs) / SEO Costs × 100

SEO costs: The total investment in SEO activities

Revenue from SEO: The total value of conversions (or sales) generated from SEO efforts
